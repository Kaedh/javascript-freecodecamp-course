### Compound Assignment With Augmented Addition.
```
   i += 5 equals i = i + 5 
```
### Compound Assignment With Augmented Subtraction.
```
   i -= 5 equals i = i - 5 
```
### Compound Assignment With Augmented Multiplication.
```
   i *= 5 equals i = i * 5 
```
### Compound Assignment With Augmented Division.
```
   i /= 5 equals i = i / 5 
```
